Singetail

General Overview

The Singetail is a medium-sized dragon belonging to the Stoker Class. Known for its versatility and overwhelming firepower, it is one of the most dangerous multi-attack dragons in existence. Unlike most dragons, the Singetail can attack from multiple parts of its body, making it unpredictable in combat and nearly impossible to approach safely. Highly territorial, it prefers to dominate its surroundings through intimidation and long-range attacks.

Physical Appearance

  • Body & Color: It has a wide, flat body covered in scales that often display bright, fiery colors such as orange, green, or yellow with spotted patterns.
  • Head & Face: Its head is compact with sharp features, and its eyes are highly flexible, capable of rotating to provide full situational awareness.
  • Appendages: It has four legs and two pairs of wings, giving it excellent balance, speed, and maneuverability both in air and on land.
  • Wings & Tail: Its double wings allow tight turns and agile flight, while its long tail is a primary weapon used to launch powerful fire attacks.

Abilities and Combat

  • Multi-Directional Fire: It can shoot fire from its mouth, tail, and even from gills along its body, making it dangerous from all angles.
  • 360° Vision: Its articulated eyes allow it to see in multiple directions at once, preventing enemies from sneaking up on it.
  • Long-Range Attacks: It can hurl fireballs from its tail, keeping opponents at a safe distance.
  • Close Combat Flames: It can release bursts of fire from its gills when enemies get too close.
  • Speed & Agility: With two pairs of wings, it is extremely agile and capable of sharp turns and rapid movement.
  • Area Control: Its ability to attack in multiple directions makes it highly effective at controlling large spaces in battle.

Behavior and Personality

  • Intelligence: It is highly aware of its surroundings and uses its vision advantage to track multiple threats at once.
  • Temperament: Singetails are extremely territorial and aggressive toward intruders.
  • Behavior: They prefer to intimidate enemies with long-range attacks before engaging directly.
  • Combat Style: They combine distance attacks with sudden close-range bursts, making them unpredictable opponents.

Behavior and Personality

  • Intelligence: They are strategic hunters that rely on awareness and positioning.
  • Temperament: They are hostile and defensive, especially when their territory is threatened.
  • Diet: Their diet consists mainly of fish and livestock typical of Stoker Class dragons.
