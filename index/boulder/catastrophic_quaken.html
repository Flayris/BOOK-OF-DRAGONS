Catastrophic Quaken

General Overview

The Catastrophic Quaken is a large dragon belonging to the Boulder Class. Known as one of the toughest and most destructive dragons in existence, it is feared for its immense strength, rock-like armor, and devastating shockwave attacks. Built like a living boulder, the Catastrophic Quaken relies on durability, raw power, and defensive rolling techniques, making it an overwhelming force in both offense and defense.

Physical Appearance

  • Body & Color: It has a massive, compact body covered in thick, pebbled hide, usually appearing in earthy shades such as brown and gray.
  • Head & Face: Its head is broad and unusual, featuring a triple-split lower jaw and a fierce, heavy-built appearance.
  • Appendages: It has strong limbs built to support its enormous weight, as well as short spikes covering much of its body.
  • Wings & Tail: Its wings are relatively small compared to its bulky frame, while its thick tail ends in a long spike used for balance and impact.

Abilities and Combat

  • Shockwave Attack: It can smash into the ground and create powerful shockwaves capable of knocking enemies out of the air or off their feet.
  • Boulder Roll: When threatened, it can curl into a tight armored ball and roll like a giant boulder, turning defense into offense.
  • Molten Lava Spit: By disgorging digested rocks, it can launch molten lava at attackers.
  • Armored Hide: Its incredibly durable skin gives it exceptional resistance against physical attacks and dragon fire.
  • Camouflage: Its rocky coloration allows it to blend naturally into cliffs, caves, and stony terrain.
  • Strength: It relies on brute force, body mass, and impact power to overpower opponents and dominate rough terrain.

Behavior and Personality

  • Intelligence: It is highly aware of danger and reacts quickly by shifting into defensive or aggressive modes.
  • Temperament: The Catastrophic Quaken is strong-willed, territorial, and often hostile when challenged.
  • Behavior: It prefers rocky environments where it can travel, feed, and use the terrain to its advantage.
  • Loyalty: Although naturally cautious and destructive, it can become caring and loyal once it learns to trust humans.
  • Diet: Their diet consists mainly of rocks, which they digest and can later spit back out as molten lava.
