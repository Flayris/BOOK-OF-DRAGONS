Hideous Zippleback

General Overview

The Hideous Zippleback is a large dragon belonging to the Mystery Class. It is one of the most unusual and dangerous dragons in existence, instantly recognizable by its two heads, each with its own mind and personality. Instead of breathing conventional fire, the Hideous Zippleback uses a deadly gas-and-spark attack, making it a highly unpredictable and explosive opponent.

Physical Appearance

  • Body & Color: It has a long, serpentine body with a cream underbelly and color variations usually centered around green, blue, or gold tones with spots.
  • Head & Face: It has two short heads at the end of two long necks, each with a large nasal horn, thinner horns on top, and yellow reptilian eyes.
  • Appendages: It has short but strong legs with four sharp claws, giving it a sturdy build despite its unusual proportions.
  • Wings & Tail: Its wings are relatively small and often appear slightly tattered, while its two tails can zip together and end in arrowhead-shaped fins.

Abilities and Combat

  • Gas and Spark: One head releases a thick green flammable gas while the other ignites it with an electrical spark, creating powerful explosions and fire walls.
  • Smokescreen Stealth: It can use its gas as cover, hiding its movements and launching ambush attacks from within the cloud.
  • Flaming Ouroboros: It can bite its own tails, ignite itself, and roll forward like a flaming wheel to strike enemies.
  • Disorienting Gas: Its gas alone can confuse and disorient humans who breathe it in.
  • Climbing Ability: It is capable of climbing effectively, especially in forested and rocky environments.
  • Strength and Coordination: When both heads work together, it becomes a highly effective fighter capable of controlling the battlefield with explosive precision.

Behavior and Personality

  • Intelligence: Each head has its own thoughts and personality, making the dragon clever but sometimes internally conflicted.
  • Temperament: Hideous Zipplebacks are tricky, evasive, and dangerous, especially when hunting or defending themselves.
  • Behavior: They often hunt through stealth and ambush, usually prowling after sundown in forests and dark places.
  • Social Traits: Although the two heads constantly argue, the species can be surprisingly social with its own kind and even capable of deep loyalty when bonded.
  • Diet: Their known diet includes fish, ham, and honeycomb.
