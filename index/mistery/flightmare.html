Flightmare

General Overview

The Flightmare is a medium-sized dragon belonging to the Mystery Class. Known for its ghost-like glow and terrifying reputation, it is one of the most mysterious dragons in the archipelago. Rather than relying on brute force, the Flightmare uses speed, defensive tactics, and a paralyzing toxic mist to immobilize its enemies, striking with precision before they can react.

Physical Appearance

  • Body & Color: It has a slender, ghost-like body covered in glowing scales and tiny spots that make it resemble a living night sky.
  • Head & Face: Its head is lined with tiny spikes and has an eerie, spectral appearance enhanced by its natural glow.
  • Appendages: Instead of ordinary spines, it is covered in tendril-like extensions along the back, head, tail, and wing tips.
  • Wings & Tail: It has translucent wings and a split tail, giving it a graceful but unsettling silhouette in flight.

Abilities and Combat

  • Paralyzing Mist: It emits a toxic mist that temporarily paralyzes victims from head to toe, leaving them helpless long enough for the dragon to strike.
  • Glowing Body: After consuming glowing algae, it can greatly intensify its body glow and use it to blind or confuse enemies.
  • Green Acid Blasts: It can also fire green acid projectiles, especially when it has not recently fed on glowing algae.
  • Speed and Agility: It is extremely fast and maneuverable, capable of keeping up with swift dragons and dodging attacks with ease.
  • Mist Trail: It can leave a lingering trail of toxic mist while flying, turning the surrounding air into a hazardous zone.
  • Lethal Spines: Mature individuals can develop deadly tail spines used for offensive purposes.

Behavior and Personality

  • Intelligence: It is highly aware of its surroundings and difficult to approach unnoticed.
  • Temperament: Flightmares are aggressive, short-tempered, and extremely territorial.
  • Behavior: They are protective of their territory and especially of their glowing algae food source, which they defend relentlessly.
  • Social Traits: They are reclusive and generally avoid contact with humans and other dragons unless provoked.
  • Diet: Their diet consists mainly of glowing algae, other algae, and fish.
