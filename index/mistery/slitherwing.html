Slitherwing

General Overview

The Slitherwing is a medium-sized dragon belonging to the Mystery Class. Resembling a giant winged serpent, it is one of the most dangerous venom-based dragons in existence. Known for its toxic body and pack-hunting behavior, the Slitherwing relies on speed, poison, and coordinated attacks to overwhelm its prey, making it a deadly predator both on land and in the air.

Physical Appearance

  • Body & Color: It has a long, snake-like body with bright warning coloration similar to venomous snakes, often featuring red, yellow, and black patterns.
  • Head & Face: Its head is lined with sharp teeth across both jaws and small spines, giving it a menacing expression.
  • Appendages: It lacks legs, relying entirely on its serpentine body for movement.
  • Wings & Tail: It has foldable wings that can tuck close to its body, allowing it to slither through tunnels, while its long tail aids balance and speed.

Abilities and Combat

  • Venomous Coating: Its entire body is covered in a toxic substance that can poison anything that comes into contact with it.
  • Poison Bite: It delivers venom through its bite, sedating prey before dragging it away.
  • Pack Hunting: Slitherwings attack in coordinated groups, overwhelming enemies with speed and numbers.
  • Extreme Speed: Its serpentine body allows it to move and strike at very high speeds, especially in confined spaces.
  • Burrowing and Ambush: It can hide in tunnels or pits, striking suddenly from below or from concealed positions.
  • Venom Resistance: Its toxic coating prevents substances like hardened amber from sticking to its body.

Behavior and Personality

  • Intelligence: It is instinct-driven but highly effective when coordinating with others in a pack.
  • Temperament: Slitherwings are extremely aggressive toward both humans and other dragons.
  • Behavior: They hunt in groups, using their venom to immobilize prey before dragging it back to their nests.
  • Social Traits: Unlike many dragons, they rely heavily on pack structure, becoming weaker when isolated.
  • Weakness: Despite being dragons, they fear fire and will retreat when exposed to it.
