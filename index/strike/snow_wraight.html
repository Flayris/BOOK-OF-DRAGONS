Snow Wraith

General Overview

The Snow Wraith is a medium-sized dragon belonging to the Strike Class. Native to icy and remote regions, it is a relentless and highly aggressive predator perfectly adapted to life in freezing environments. Known for its ability to hunt in complete whiteout conditions, the Snow Wraith relies less on sight and more on advanced thermal sensing, making it one of the most dangerous dragons in blizzards.

Physical Appearance

  • Body & Color: It has a long, sleek body covered in pale white and icy blue scales, allowing it to blend seamlessly into snowy landscapes and remain nearly invisible in blizzards.
  • Head & Face: Its head features sharp, predatory traits with pointed teeth and two prominent tusks on its lower jaw, giving it a menacing and animalistic appearance.
  • Appendages: The Snow Wraith has wing claws and strong limbs adapted for gripping ice and snow, making it effective both in flight and on the ground.
  • Wings & Tail: It possesses powerful wings for fast flight through harsh storms, and a flexible tail used for balance, control, and striking during combat.

Abilities and Combat

  • Thermal Vision: Due to its poor eyesight, it uses thermal vision to detect heat signatures, allowing it to track prey even in complete blizzards or darkness.
  • Frost Blasts: It can fire rapid, concentrated blasts of icy energy capable of knocking ◄ BACK or freezing targets with strong impact force.
  • Camouflage: Its white coloration makes it nearly invisible in snowy environments, giving it a major advantage when ambushing prey.
  • Burrowing & Ambush: It can tunnel through snow and ice, allowing it to hide beneath the surface and attack unexpectedly.
  • Speed & Agility: As a Strike Class dragon, it is extremely fast and agile, capable of navigating powerful blizzards with ease.
  • Cold Resistance: It thrives in sub-zero temperatures and uses extreme cold environments as its natural hunting ground.

Behavior and Personality

  • Intelligence: It is highly intelligent and strategic, capable of using environmental conditions like blizzards to its advantage during hunts.
  • Temperament: The Snow Wraith is extremely aggressive, relentless, and territorial, making it a very dangerous opponent.
  • Behavior: It is often a solitary or pack hunter that prefers isolation in frozen regions, striking without warning from beneath the snow.
  • Hunting Style: It relies on stealth, patience, and sudden ambushes, attacking when prey is disoriented or unable to see through harsh weather conditions.
